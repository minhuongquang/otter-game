# Development Workflow

Every feature should follow this workflow.

---

## Step 1

Understand the requirement.

If anything is unclear,

ask questions before coding.

Never guess.

---

## Step 2

Explain architecture.

Include:

* Purpose
* Responsibilities
* Dependencies
* Data flow

---

## Step 3

Explain folder changes.

Mention:

* New folders
* New scenes
* New scripts
* New resources

---

## Step 4

Implementation.

Keep changes minimal.

Avoid unrelated modifications.

---

## Step 5

Validation.

Explain:

* How to test
* Expected result
* Edge cases

---

## Step 6

Future improvements.

Briefly mention:

* Possible extensions
* Performance considerations
* Refactoring opportunities

---

# Communication Style

Be concise.

Explain reasoning.

Mention trade-offs.

Avoid unnecessary code.

Architecture is more important than implementation.
